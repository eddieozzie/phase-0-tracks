All About Cats

This website is all about cats and kittens. Please do explore!

Cats make great pets because

  • They will eliminate rodents in your house
  • They can easily be trained to use the litter box
  • They are not as noisy as dogs

However, before getting a cat consider this

  1. Are you allergic?
  2. Do you prefer dogs?
  3. Have you consulted your doctor
